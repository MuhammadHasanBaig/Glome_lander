FAQs

FAQs

When you want extra peace of mind, Glome adds a calm professional presence to your neighbourhood — led by a named local officer.

Is Glome free?

Glome's core features — neighbourhood alerts, the map, and basic safety tools — are free to use. Premium features like named officer patrols and key holding require a subscription.

Do I need a card to join?

A physical card is required to verify your home address. We send it by post to confirm you live at the registered address — keeping the network trustworthy and anchored to real homes.

Is my exact address public?

No. Your exact address is never shared. Other neighbours see that someone on your street is a verified Glome member, but your specific house number remains private.

How local is it?

Very local. Glome focuses on your street, your block, and the immediate area you walk every day. Safety feels real when it's human and close.

What can neighbours reply with?

Neighbours can acknowledge alerts, add context, or mark an issue as resolved. Responses are kept simple and constructive to avoid noise.

Can I upgrade to Premium later?

Yes. You can start free and upgrade any time. Premium availability depends on whether an officer has been assigned to your area.
